CHOOSE Select one or more bottles to taste. Works on any bottle with natural cork.
ACCESS Quickly press the trigger to clear the needle of oxygen. Push the needle down through the cork, to access the wine.
POUR Tilt the bottle as you would to pour normally. Press and release trigger to pour wine. Repeat when flow slows or stops. Return bottle to upright position.
ENJOY The cork naturally reseals to form a perfect seal, never allowing oxygen to touch the wine. The wine remaining in the bottle is preserved so you can continue to enjoy it for months or years to come.
